1. Project Overview

IPIX CRM is a custom-built web application designed exclusively for Nikshan Electronics. It replaces manual spreadsheets and disconnected tools with a single unified platform that handles the entire customer lifecycle — from the first lead to after-sales service.

The system supports multiple branches (stores), multiple user roles, and covers sales with GST billing, EMI financing, delivery tracking, service requests, warranty management, and WhatsApp notifications — all in one place.

Multi-Branch Multi-Role GST Compliant WhatsApp Ready EMI Tracking Warranty Alerts Real-time Data

5. Key Features Explained

5.1 GST Billing System

Every product has a GST percentage (0%, 5%, 12%, 18%, 28%) stored in the product database. When a sale is made:

With GSTIN
  • Store GSTIN number is printed on every invoice
  • Invoice shows subtotal, GST breakup by rate, and grand total
  • All GST data is saved to the database for accounting

5.2 Split Payment

Customers can pay using two payment methods in a single transaction — e.g., part cash + part card, or part UPI + part EMI.

  • Enable "Split Payment" toggle in the cart
  • Choose Payment Method 1 + Amount 1
  • Choose Payment Method 2 + Amount 2
  • System validates: Amount 1 + Amount 2 must equal Grand Total
  • Both payments are saved and shown on the invoice
Cash Card UPI EMI Cheque Gift / Free

5.3 WhatsApp Integration

One-click WhatsApp messages are sent directly to the customer's phone without any third-party API cost. It opens WhatsApp with a pre-filled message.

Trigger Message Sent
After Purchase Purchase receipt with product details, amount, and store contact
Service Resolved "Your device is ready for pickup" notification
Warranty Expiring Warranty reminder with expiry date and service offer
Delivery Done Delivery confirmation with thank you message

5.4 Warranty Tracker

Each product has a warranty period (in months). When a sale is made, the warranty expiry date is automatically calculated and stored.

✅ IN WARRANTY — Green badge ⚠️ EXPIRING SOON — Orange (within 30 days) ❌ OUT OF WARRANTY — Red badge
  • Visible on Customer Detail page under each purchase
  • Visible on Service Request form — auto-checks warranty when customer is selected
  • "Remind via WhatsApp" button appears for expiring warranties

5.5 Delivery Tracking

A dedicated delivery management page tracks all product deliveries from the store to the customer's address.

⚠️ Overdue deliveries are highlighted in red automatically

5.6 Date Filters (All Pages)

Every list page has a consistent date filter bar allowing staff to quickly filter records by time period.

Today This Week This Month Last Month Custom Range Clear

Available on: Purchases, Service Requests, EMI Tracker, Leads, Follow-ups, Deliveries

5.7 Return / Replacement Policy Checker

When a service request is created and the request type is "Return" or "Replacement", the system automatically checks:

  • Return window: 15 days from purchase date
  • Replacement window: 30 days from purchase date
  • Shows ✅ "Within window", ⚠️ "Last Day", or ❌ "Window closed"

6. Database Structure

The database is hosted on Supabase (PostgreSQL). All tables follow the prefix nk_ for Nikshan.

Table Purpose Key Fields
nk_customers All customer profiles name, phone, email, address, loyalty_points, referred_by
nk_products Product catalog name, model_no, brand, price, gst_percentage, warranty_months, stock_count
nk_stores Branch/store info name, address, phone, gstin
nk_users Staff accounts name, email, role, store_id, active, last_login
nk_purchases Individual product sale lines product_id, final_price, gst_percentage, gst_amount, warranty_expiry
nk_invoices Full sale invoice payment_type, total_amount, gst_total, split_payment, amount1, amount2
nk_emi_records EMI financing records bank_name, tenure_months, monthly_emi, start_date, status
nk_service_requests Repair / service tickets issue, status, technician_id, resolved_at, whatsapp_sent
nk_leads Potential customers name, phone, source, interest, status, assigned_to
nk_followups Follow-up schedule customer_id, due_date, notes, status (pending/done/missed)
nk_deliveries Delivery tracking invoice_id, address, scheduled_date, status, delivered_at
nk_activity_log Customer interaction history customer_id, action_type, notes, created_by, created_at
nk_targets Sales targets per staff/store user_id, store_id, month, year, target_amount, target_type
